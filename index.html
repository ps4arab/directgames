How to download and install games directly to your jailbroken ps4:

 

1- connect your ps4 to internet.

2- open the game link on your ps4 browser.

3- press IAM A HUMAN button.

4- wait 3 seconds.

5- press get link.

6- close any additional opened windows.

7- place the mouse cursor on your browser at the link that showed up.

8- long press with X button on the first letter of the link that appeard.

9 - press the three button bellow the space button on your virtual keyboard.

10 - press select.

11 - select the whole link.

12- press X.

13- press copy.

14- close the browser.

15- open ps4 xplorer 1.34

16- go to user/download.

17- press triangle.

18- press download url here.

19- delete the http://

20- press the three button bellow the space button on your virtual keyboard.

21- press paste

22- press done

23- wait for the download to complete.

24- after the download is complete press triangle on the file and press rename then add .pkg at the end of its name and press done

25- install the pkg file.
